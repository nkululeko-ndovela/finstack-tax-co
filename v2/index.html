What we do

Five services, one filing cabinet.

Hand us one part of your finances or all of it — most clients start with bookkeeping and tax, then add the rest once they see how we work.

01

Bookkeeping

Your books, kept current instead of catching up.

  • Recording daily sales and income
  • Tracking and categorising expenses
  • Organising and reconciling bank records
02

Tax Preparation

From the yearly return to the SARS letter you've been avoiding.

  • Filing individual and business tax returns
  • Tax planning to legally lower your bill
  • Support and representation during audits
03

Financial Statements

Reports you can hand to a bank without a second thought.

  • Balance sheets
  • Income statements
  • Cash flow reports
04

Payroll Processing

Staff paid correctly, paperwork filed without chasing.

  • Calculating employee pay accurately
  • Withholding the correct PAYE, UIF and SDL
  • Filing payroll reports (EMP201 / EMP501)
05

Business Advisory

Someone to talk numbers with before the big decisions, not after.

  • Financial planning built around your goals
  • Budgeting and cash flow forecasting
  • Guidance on business growth choices
