What we do

Five services, handled by one team.

Bring us one part of your finances or all of it. Most clients start with bookkeeping and tax, then add payroll or advisory once they see how we work.

Account 01Daily operations

Bookkeeping

Clean, current books that reflect what's actually happening in your business.
  • Recording daily sales and income
  • Tracking and categorising expenses
  • Organising and reconciling bank records
Account 02Annual & SARS

Tax Preparation

From your yearly return to that SARS letter you've been putting off. We handle it and keep you on time.
  • Filing individual and business tax returns
  • Tax planning to legally lower your tax bill
  • Representation and support during tax audits
Account 03Reporting

Financial Statements

Statements you can hand to a bank or an investor without a second thought.
  • Balance sheets
  • Income statements
  • Cash flow reports

Payroll Processing

Your staff get paid correctly, and the paperwork gets filed without you chasing it.
  • Calculating employee pay accurately
  • Withholding the correct PAYE, UIF and SDL
  • Filing payroll reports (EMP201 / EMP501)

Business Advisory

Someone to talk numbers with before you make the big calls, not just after.
  • Financial planning built around your goals
  • Budgeting and cash flow forecasting
  • Guidance on business growth choices
